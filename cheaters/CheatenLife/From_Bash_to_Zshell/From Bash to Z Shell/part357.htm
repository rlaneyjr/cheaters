Propagating Functions

Once you have built up a good collection of functions, you will come across the issue of where to store them in a tidy fashion and how you make them available. Initially, the obvious place to put them is your startup file or files sourced from your startup files. For small functions this is fine, but if you have a great many functions, it can take surprisingly long to read them all in. In this section, we will show you some ways of managing them better.
