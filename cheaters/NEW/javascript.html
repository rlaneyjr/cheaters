Regular Expressions Syntax


^

Start of string

$ End of string

.

Any single character

(a|b)

a or b

(...)

Group section

[abc]

In range (a, b or c)

[^abc]

Not in range

\s

White space

a?

Zero or one of a

a*

Zero or more of a

a*?

Zero or more, ungreedy

a+

One or more of a

a+?

One or more, ungreedy

a{3}

Exactly 3 of a

a{3,}

3 or more of a

a{,6}

Up to 6 of a

a{3,6}

3 to 6 of a

a{3,6}?

3 to 6 of a, ungreedy

\

Escape character

[:punct:]

Any punctuation symbol

[:space:]

Any space character

[:blank:]

Space or tab

There's an excellent regular expression tester at: http://regexpal.com/


Pattern Modifiers


g

Global match

i *

Case-insensitive

m *

Multiple lines

s *

Treat string as single line
